Welcome

Welcome to my website. My aim for it is to provide a place for me to get my work and ideas out-there quickly and link together my outputs across the internet into a single place. Enjoy your visit!

About me

I’m a geographer and environmental scientist specialising in spatial data analysis and R programming. Based at the Institute for Transport Studies (ITS) I have a growing interest in transport modelling, as illustrated by a book review of a book on the subject Boyce and Williams (2015).

I studied Geography at the University of Bristol after moving out of my home town of Hereford where I went to Weobley High School out in rural northwest Herefordshire and the Hereford Sixth Form College.

From an early age I enjoyed travelling. Between moving to Bristol when I left home to settling in Leeds for my current job I moved 3 times:

  • To Salamanca in 2006 for a year studying at the Universidad de Salamanca on the Erasmus scheme, where I learned Spanish.

  • To York in 2008, where I was awarded a scholarship to do a master’s degree in Environmental Science and Management at the University of York.

  • To Sheffield in 2009, where I was awarded a PhD scholarship to research Energy Futures at the University of Sheffield as part of the E-Futures Doctoral Training Centre.

I moved (by bicycle trailer!) from Sheffield to Leeds in summer 2013 for to take a post-doctorate fellowship in Geospatial Data Analysis and Simulation (TALISMAN). A year later I got a job at the Leeds Institute for Data Analytics (LIDA) where I am still based alongside my role in ITS.

I have been based at the Leeds Institute for Transport Studies (ITS) since September 2016 as a University Academic Fellow in Transport and Big Data. Here work on researching the energy impacts of transport systems, modelling sustainable transport uptake and visualizing transport futures.

Applied work

I’ve applied my skills to a number of projects with real-world applications, most recently the Propensity to Cycle Tool (Lovelace et al. 2017), a nationally scalable interactive online mapping application for the Department for Transport.

I also develop the stplanr package for sustainable transport planning.
